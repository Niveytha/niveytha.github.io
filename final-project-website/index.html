WELCOME TO MY FINAL PROJECT STORYBOARD!
MOTIVATIONS I'm passionate about the genre of horror as well as mystery puzzle games and want to challenge myself to create a similar game. By working on this project, I hope to improve my programming skills and learn more about game development. Additionally, I want to share my love for these types of games with others and provide them with an engaging and entertaining experience.
STORYLINE As the lead detective, you have been tasked with solving the murder of Mr. Smith. However, the suspects are not making it easy for you. His wife appears calm but might be hiding something, the butler is acting suspiciously, and his business partner has a history of shady deals. You must explore the many rooms of the mansion, gather clues, and interrogate the suspects to solve the case. Every decision you make could take you closer to the killer or down a dangerous path, with multiple endings waiting for you to uncover. Are you ready to step into the shoes of a detective and crack the case?

DATA GATHERED The game will collect data on the player's choices and decisions throughout the game, including which clues they collect, which characters they talk to, and who they accuse of the murder. This data will be used to determine which ending the player receives.
AGENDAS - Explore locations and collect clues
- Interact with characters and objects for hidden clues
- Solve puzzles and mini-games for more clues
- Use clues to identify the murderer
- Choose the correct culprit to win
