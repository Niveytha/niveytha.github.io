PROJECT WEBSITE Questions for Week 10 GAME WEBSITE
What does the game do? The game is a murder mystery game in which the player takes on the role of the lead detective tasked with solving the murder of Mr. Smith. The player must explore the various rooms of the mansion, gather clues, and interrogate the suspects to solve the case. The suspects include Mr. Smith's wife, who appears calm but may be hiding something, the butler who is acting suspiciously, and the business partner who has a history of shady deals. The player must use their detective skills to navigate through the clues and interrogations, making decisions that can either take them closer to the killer or down a dangerous path. The game has multiple endings, which are dependent on the player's choices and decisions made throughout the game.
What are the rules it follows? Can you write down those rules? 1. The player can move in four directions (up, down, left, right) using the arrow keys.
2. The player must explore each room and interact with characters to gather clues.
3. The player can interact with objects by walking into them.
4. The player must use the clues/information gathered to solve the murder mystery.
5. The player is unable to discern who the killer is without all the necessary clues.
6. The game is over when the player reaches the final screen.
What can the user do? The user can:
1. Explore the mansion and its various rooms
2. Interact with objects and search for clues
3. Talk to suspects and interrogate them for information
4. Make decisions that will affect the outcome of the game
5. Solve puzzles and challenges to progress through the game.
What does the program do for each user action? For each user action, the program responds by:
- Moving the player character in the chosen direction.
- Checking for collisions between the player character and objects.
- If the player character collides with an object, the program triggers an event associated with that object.
- If the player collects an item, it is added to the player's inventory.
- If the player uses an item, it may trigger an event in the game that advances the story or helps the player solve a puzzle.
When does the game end? The game ends when the player collects all the necessary clues and reaches the final screen, where they are presented with a list of suspects and must decide on the killer. The player needs to consider all the evidence they have gathered throughout the game to make an informed decision. If they choose the right killer, they will have successfully completed the game. However, if they choose the wrong killer, they will fail and the game will end. This adds pressure to the player's decision-making process and makes the game more exciting. When the player successfully completes the game, they feel a sense of satisfaction and accomplishment for having solved the murder mystery.
